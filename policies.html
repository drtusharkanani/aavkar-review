Who We Are

GoodReview ("we", "our", "us") is a SaaS platform that helps Indian doctors collect Google reviews. We operate at goodreview.in. Our support contact is support.goodreview@gmail.com.

What Data We Collect

When a doctor registers on GoodReview, we collect:

  • Full name, degree, and medical specialty
  • Hospital or clinic name, city, area, and state
  • WhatsApp number and email address
  • Google My Business (GMB) review link
  • Selected plan and payment information (processed by Razorpay)
  • Delivery address (for physical product plans only)

When a patient uses a doctor's review page, we do not collect any patient personal data. No login, no registration, no tracking of patients.

How We Use Your Data
  • To create and maintain your GoodReview doctor profile
  • To send your review page link and activation confirmation via email
  • To deliver physical products (NFC, standee, QR stickers)
  • To send renewal reminders before plan expiry
  • To provide customer support via WhatsApp
  • To improve our platform and services
Data Storage

All doctor profile data is stored securely in Airtable (US-based cloud database). Payment data is handled entirely by Razorpay — we do not store credit card or UPI details on our servers.

🔒 We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for marketing purposes.
Third Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

  • Razorpay — Payment processing
  • Airtable — Data storage
  • Make.com — Automation workflows
  • Gmail — Transactional emails
  • GitHub Pages — Website hosting
  • Google Maps API — GMB link integration
Your Rights
